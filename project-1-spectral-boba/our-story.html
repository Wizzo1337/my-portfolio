Our Story

Our passion and purpose is to bring the best tasting bubble tea to Fountain Valley

Our founders grew up right here in Fountain Valley and were tired of drinking mediocre tea from stores that valued saving money over taste. They then decided to take a trip to Vietnam where they toured the whole country tasting many different types of teas and decided on the perfect tea leaves to bring back here

Their hard work has paid off as they have opened numerous stores in various states including Texas, Hawaii, and Nevada. They now want to share their awesome tea with the people in Fountain Valley.
